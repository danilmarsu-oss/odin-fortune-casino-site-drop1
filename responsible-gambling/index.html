Recognising Problem Gambling

It is important to be aware of the signs that gambling may be becoming a problem. At Odin Fortune Casino, we encourage all of our players to regularly assess their gambling behaviour and seek help if they notice any of the following warning signs:

  • Spending more money on gambling than you can afford to lose
  • Chasing losses in an attempt to win back money you have already lost
  • Gambling interfering with your work, relationships, or daily responsibilities
  • Feeling anxious, irritable, or restless when you are not gambling
  • Borrowing money or selling possessions to fund your gambling
  • Lying to friends or family about the amount of time or money you spend gambling
  • Using gambling as a way to escape from stress, anxiety, or other personal problems
  • Feeling unable to stop gambling even when you want to

If you recognise any of these signs in yourself or someone you know, we strongly encourage you to reach out for support. You are never alone, and help is always available.
